Our Story

We Are Wild About the Outdoors

Wildman Hunting & Outdoor Robertson is your local one-stop destination for everything hunting, shooting, fishing and outdoor adventure. Situated at 8 Voortrekker Ave in the heart of Robertson, we proudly serve the Breede River Valley and surrounding communities.

We stock a carefully selected range of firearms, ammunition, fishing equipment, camping gear, outdoor clothing and knives — sourced from trusted brands and chosen by people who actually use the gear in the field.

Our team is passionate about the outdoors. Whether you are a first-time hunter looking for your first licence, a seasoned angler seeking the right tackle for the Breede River, or a family setting up their first camping trip — we are here to help with honest, practical advice and no pressure.

We are proud to be part of the Robertson community and look forward to seeing you in-store.
