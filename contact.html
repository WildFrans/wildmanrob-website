Common Questions

Do you offer layby or payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans over 3, 6 or 12 months on firearms and gear. Come in-store or call us to arrange.

Can you help me with my firearm licence application?

Absolutely. We assist with competency certificates, new licence applications, renewals and NARFO registration. Visit us in-store and we'll guide you through the process.

Do you buy or trade in second-hand firearms?

Yes, we accept trade-ins and purchase second-hand firearms. Bring yours in for a fair, honest valuation. All transfers are handled legally.

Do you stock a specific brand or calibre I'm looking for?

We carry a wide range but not everything is always in stock. WhatsApp us or call and we'll check availability or arrange a special order for you.

Can I pay online?

Online payment is coming soon. Currently we accept cash, card (EFT/debit/credit) in-store and can arrange deposit payments for layby or special orders.
