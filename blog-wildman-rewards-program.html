Join Our Rewards Program — Earn on Every Purchase

Shop News June 2026 2 min read

Regulars, this one's for you: the Wildman rewards program is live in-store. Every rand you spend earns you Wildman Moolah — and Moolah is as good as cash at the counter.

How it works

  1. Sign up in-store — it takes two minutes at the till and membership is free.
  2. Earn on every purchase — ammunition, gear, tackle, clothing, the lot.
  3. Spend your Moolah like cash — put it toward your next box of ammo, that scope you've been eyeing, or the kids' fishing rods.

Why we run it

Simple: Robertson looks after us, so we look after Robertson. Our regulars buy their ammo, licences, tackle and gear here all year — the rewards program is our way of making sure loyalty pays in both directions.
